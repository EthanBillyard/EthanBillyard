About me:

I created my first video in 2004. However it was in 2014 that I took my first photography and film class at Hunting Hills High School. It was there that I learned how to properly compose a shot and begin editing. I continued my education until graduation in 2018. During that time I specialized in suspenseful films with the occasional comedy video. In 2018 I was able to compete in the Skills Canada Competition where I won second place in the provincial competitions. In 2019 I pursued online automotive marketing and began using my skills to aid in the sale of vehicles and related merchandise. In an effort to continue my education I returned to Red Deer Polytechnic to enroll in the Computer Programming course.
