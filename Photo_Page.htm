Photography Experience:

Photography has always been more of a passion for me than a means to make money. However, over the last six years I have occasionally taken on paid projects including weddings, photo shoots for social media, and stock photography for vehicles. Macro photography is my favourite style to experiment with because the small details in everyday objects are often overlooked, and a keen eye can show those details in unique and interesting ways.
